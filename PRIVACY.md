# Privacy Policy - Spin4Pi

**Last Updated:** January 10, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Spin4Pi ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information from Pi Network

When you authenticate with Pi Network, we receive:
- Your Pi Network username
- Your Pi Network user ID (UID)
- Access token for session management

We do **NOT** have access to:
- Your Pi wallet private keys
- Your Pi wallet balance
- Your personal identification documents

### 2.2 Information We Generate

Through your use of the App, we collect:
- Spin history and results
- Win/loss statistics
- NFT ownership records
- VIP membership status
- Tournament participation data
- Achievement progress
- Login streak information

### 2.3 Optional Information

You may choose to provide:
- Email address (for notifications)
- Notification preferences

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the App
- Process spins and payments
- Track leaderboard rankings
- Manage NFT ownership
- Administer VIP memberships
- Send streak reminders (if opted in)
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Improve our services
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Storage
Your data is stored securely using industry-standard cloud infrastructure (Supabase/Lovable Cloud).

### 4.2 Security Measures
We implement:
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Row-level security policies
- Server-side validation
- Secure payment processing

### 4.3 Retention
We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.

## 5. Data Sharing

### 5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data
We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

### 5.2 Third-Party Services
We may share data with:
- **Pi Network**: For authentication and payments
- **Email Service Provider**: For sending notifications (Resend)
- **Analytics**: Anonymized usage statistics

### 5.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

## 6. Cookies and Tracking

The App uses:
- Local storage for session management
- No third-party tracking cookies
- Pi Ads SDK (subject to Pi Network's privacy policy)

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request data correction
- Request data deletion
- Opt out of notifications
- Export your data

To exercise these rights, contact us through the Pi Network App platform.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Spin4Pi is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 9. International Users

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

## 10. Pi Network Compliance

We comply with all Pi Network privacy and data handling requirements. Our data practices are designed to align with Pi Network ecosystem standards.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App.

## 12. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns, contact us through the Pi Network App platform.
